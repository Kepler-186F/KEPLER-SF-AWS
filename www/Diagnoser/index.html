Specialized Right at your figure tips

When diarrhea happens to you, DiarrheaDiagnoser gives you information whether you may stay at home to see if it gets better OR you need to see doctor right away. Diarrhea accounts for 1.5 Million outpatient visits every year in States but many of them is self-diagnosable and self-curable. If you have some basic information available, such as the position of abdominal pain and stool volume, DiarrheaDiagnoser can list most common risks that fit your symptoms and give you some basic advices correspondingly. On the other hand, diarrhea can be life threating. DiarrheaDiagnoser also build in a module to capture the sign of some common lethal diseases at their early stages such as chorale and food poisoning. Please be advised that DiarrheaDiagnoser is not a clinical diagnosis alternative, reliance on the information provided by DiarrheaDiagnoser will be at your own risk.

Free to Use. Free to distribute.

DiarrheaDiagnoser is a free-of-charge application. DiarrheaDiagnoser will not collect nor track any user's information. Also, Diarrhea Diagnoser is an 100% open source project. We welcome and encourage more developer to jump in and contribute.
