First of its kind. Start to experience the new way of reading news.

Use it like your regular news page or RSS dashboard. Except that, for every serch term you entered, Kepler View crawling through different contries' internet acorssing different langurages. After the targets were found, Kepler View will translate all of them to your langurage and present to you side by side. With only one click, all complexities were handled at back.

Amazingly Designed. Beautifully Done.

Crawling through hundreds langurages and million articles around the world is challenging. In order to deliver the most accurate result in the shortest time, we desiged the brand new natual langurage crawler MULLER NLCS 2nd Gen. as well as our so-far fastest Hadoop™ based content warehousing system KEPLER HDDS, the one also powering other projects including our famous Kepler Crawler project.

Free to Use. Free to distribute.

Kepler View, also known as View of Kepler(VOK), is a free-of-charge service. Kepler View will not collect nor track user's information. Also, VOK is an open source project which we welcome and encourage more developer to jump in and contribute.
